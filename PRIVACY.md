## Introduction

The AOS Evidence Repository ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit **aos-evidence.com**.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Analytics Data (Optional)
If you consent to analytics, we collect:
- **Page views** and navigation patterns
- **Referring websites**
- **Browser type** and operating system
- **Approximate location** (country/city level)
- **Time spent** on pages

**Tool Used:** Matomo Analytics (self-hosted at `analytics.git-cathedral.com`)

### 2. Technical Data (Automatic)
- **IP addresses** (anonymized after 24 hours)
- **Browser fingerprints** (for security)
- **Error logs** (for debugging)

### 3. No Personal Data
We **DO NOT** collect:
- Names, emails, or contact information
- Payment information
- Social media profiles
- Personally identifiable information (PII)
