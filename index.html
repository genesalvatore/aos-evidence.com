About AOS

Constitutional governance framework for artificial intelligence

What is AOS?

AOS (Agent Operating System) is a constitutional governance framework that provides deterministic enforcement of AI behavioral constraints. Unlike training-based approaches that make violations less likely, AOS makes violations mathematically detectable and cryptographically provable.

Key Innovation

First system to combine cryptographic attestation, immutable audit trails, and OS-level enforcement to create verifiable AI governance. Training creates aligned models; enforcement provides mathematical guarantees. Together: defense-in-depth for AI safety.

Transparency Principle

This repository embodies that principle: complete disclosure, independent verification, falsifiable claims, and permanent public record.
